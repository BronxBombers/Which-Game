When you own hundreds of Steam games, finding the perfect game for your current mood can be difficult! Well, fortunately for you, which game is a tool designed to help you decide which game to play if you the plight of owning too many steam games. The tool utilizes a quick survey, which is backed by loads of science, to condense how you are feeling and what your environment is like, into a few key words. The tool then performs a search of your steam library for games to perfectly match your mood! If you feel the survey misrepresents your mood do not fret! There are also preset moods to choose from.
